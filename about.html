🏢 Who We Are

Building Dreams, One Home at a Time

Founded in 2008 in the heart of Bhubaneswar, Compass Housing Society began as a small team of passionate real estate professionals with one mission: to make property buying honest, transparent, and accessible to every Indian family.

Today, we are Odisha's most trusted real estate platform — serving 15,000+ families, managing 850+ active listings, and closing deals worth over ₹1,200 crores across India.

📅 Our Journey

16 Years of Excellence

08

2008 — Founded

Compass Housing Society established in Bhubaneswar with a team of 5 agents and a vision to transform real estate in Odisha.

11

2011 — First 1,000 Clients

Reached our milestone of 1,000 happy families. Expanded services to Cuttack and Puri districts.

15

2015 — Digital Transformation

Launched our online portal and mobile app. Introduced virtual property tours and digital documentation.

18

2018 — Expansion

Expanded to 8 cities across India. Launched commercial property division and NRI investment services.

22

2022 — AI Integration

Introduced AI-powered property matching and market analytics tools for smarter homebuying decisions.

24

2024 — 15K Families Served

Celebrated 15,000+ happy families and ₹1,200+ Crore in property transactions. Odisha's #1 real estate platform.
